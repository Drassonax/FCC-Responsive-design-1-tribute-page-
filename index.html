Leopold Ružička

Leopold Ružička in his laboratory

    Timeline of Leopold Ružička's life:

  • 1887: Born in Vukovar, modern day Croatia, then part of Austro-Hungarian Empire
  • 1910: Obtained his doctoral degree and move to Zurich to work with Hermann Staudinger, a German organic chemist
  • 1916: Started working with Haarman & Reimer, the oldest perfume manufacturer in the world, due to his expertise in the terpene field
  • 1930: Became professor of organic chemistry at ETH in Zurich and widened the area of his research, adding to it the chemistry of higher terpenes and steroids
  • 1939: Won the Nobel prize for chemistry after the successful synthesis of sex hormones (androsterone and testosterone)
  • 1976: Died in Mammern, Switzerland
